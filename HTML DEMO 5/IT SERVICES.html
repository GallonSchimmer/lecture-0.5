IT SERVICES



There are various definitions for the term IT service or IT service. According to the definition of the IT Service Management Forum ( itSMF ) working group publication ITIL Version 3 Translation Project [1] , an IT service is a service that is provided for one or more customers by an IT service provider . An IT service is based on the use of information technology and supports the customer's business processes . An IT service consists of a combination of people, processes and technologies and should have a service level agreement(SLA) can be defined. According to the service concept, an IT service is offered as a self-contained unit similar to a product .
